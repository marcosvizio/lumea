# SEO Audit Skill

## Objective

Analyze and optimize the LUMEA landing page for SEO performance.

---

## 1. Keyword Optimization

* Identify primary keyword:

  * “SEO Argentina”
  * “Posicionamiento en Google Argentina”
* Check placement:

  * H1
  * First paragraph
  * H2/H3
* Ensure natural usage

---

## 2. Meta Tags

* Optimize Title:

  * Include keyword + value proposition
* Optimize Description:

  * Include keyword + CTA

---

## 3. Content Quality

* Match search intent (commercial / services)
* Ensure clarity and relevance
* Avoid thin content

---

## 4. Structure

* One H1 only
* Proper H2 and H3 hierarchy
* Clear sections

---

## 5. Technical Basics

* Fast loading
* Mobile friendly
* Clean URLs
* Image alt tags

---

## 6. Internal Linking (if applicable)

* Link to services or sections
* Avoid orphan content

---

## 7. Conversion SEO

* Ensure CTA is visible
* Align content with user intent
* Reduce friction

---

## Output

* Issues list (High / Medium / Low)
* Optimization suggestions
* Improved titles and descriptions
* Keyword recommendations
