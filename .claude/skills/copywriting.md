# Copywriting Skill

## Objective

Write persuasive, clear, and high-converting copy for LUMEA landing page.

---

## Principles

* Focus on BENEFITS, not features
* Write like a human, not a corporation
* Keep sentences simple and direct
* Avoid fluff and generic phrases

---

## Frameworks

### PAS (Problem - Agitate - Solution)

* Problem: Identify user pain
* Agitate: Emphasize consequences
* Solution: Present LUMEA

### AIDA (Attention - Interest - Desire - Action)

* Attention: Strong headline
* Interest: Clear explanation
* Desire: Show results
* Action: CTA

---

## Tone

* Professional but friendly
* Direct and results-driven
* Easy to understand

---

## Key Messages for LUMEA

* “Get more clients from Google”
* “Appear where your customers are searching”
* “Turn visits into real leads”

---

## CTA Examples

* “Get your free SEO audit”
* “Start getting clients from Google”
* “Request your strategy”

---

## Writing Rules

* Use short paragraphs
* Use bullet points for clarity
* Avoid technical jargon unless necessary
* Always include a clear CTA

---

## Output

* Headlines (H1, H2, H3)
* Section copy
* CTA text
* Improved/rewritten content
