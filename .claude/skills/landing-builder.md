# Landing Builder Skill

## Objective

Create a high-converting landing page for LUMEA services focused on SEO, Google positioning, and lead generation.

## Target Audience

* Small and medium business owners
* Local businesses in Argentina
* People who want more clients from Google

## Core Goal

Generate leads (form submissions, WhatsApp clicks, calls)

---

## Structure

### 1. Hero Section

* Clear value proposition (what + result)
* Include primary keyword (SEO / posicionamiento Google)
* Short supporting sentence
* Strong CTA (Get a quote / Auditoría gratis)

### 2. Problem Section

* Describe pain points:

  * “No apareces en Google”
  * “No recibís consultas”
  * “Tu competencia te supera”
* Make it relatable and direct

### 3. Solution Section

* Present LUMEA as the solution
* Explain clearly:

  * SEO optimization
  * Google Business optimization
  * Landing pages

### 4. Benefits Section

* Focus on RESULTS:

  * More visibility
  * More leads
  * Better conversion
* Use bullet points

### 5. Services Breakdown

* SEO
* Google Business
* Analytics
* Landing Pages

### 6. Social Proof (optional but recommended)

* Testimonials
* Metrics (if available)

### 7. CTA Section

* Repeat CTA
* Reduce friction (free audit, no commitment)

---

## SEO Rules

* Use keyword in H1
* Use variations in H2/H3
* Avoid keyword stuffing
* Optimize meta title + description

---

## Conversion Rules

* Always write benefit-driven content
* Avoid generic phrases
* Use simple language
* Every section must push toward action

---

## Output

* Full landing page structure
* Section-by-section content
* Suggested headlines and CTAs
